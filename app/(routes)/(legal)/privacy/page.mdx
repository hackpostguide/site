# Hackpost Guide Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 4/25/24

At Hackpost Guide, we understand the importance of privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and handle your personal information when you use our services, including any content you create or information that you provide on our platform.

### What Information Do We Collect?

- **Account Information:** When you create an account, we collect your username and name.
- **User-Generated Content:** We collect and store the content that you create, post, or share on Hackpost Guide, including bios, projects, comments, and other participatory content.
- **Interaction Data:** We collect information about your interactions with the service, which helps us improve Hackpost Guide.

### How Do We Use Your Information?

- **To Provide and Manage Services:** We use your information to operate, maintain, and provide the features and functionality of Hackpost Guide.
- **Improvements:** We use the information to understand and analyze the usage trends and preferences of our users, to improve Hackpost Guide, and to develop new products, services, features, and functionality.
- **Communication:** We may use your information to communicate with you, including sending service-related notices.

### How Is Your Information Shared?

- **User Interactions:** Any information you post publicly is visible to others. Users can see your username and any content you post.
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect and defend the rights or property of Hackpost Guide, act in urgent circumstances to protect the personal safety of users of Hackpost Guide, or protect against legal liability.

### Your Choices

- **Accessing and Updating Your Information:** You can review and edit your personal information at any time by logging into your account on Hackpost Guide.
- **Deletion:** You can request to delete your account by [instructions on how users can delete their accounts].

### Children's Privacy

Hackpost Guide is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13 or knowingly allow such persons to register for the service. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under age 13 without verification of parental consent, we take steps to remove that information.

### Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information that we collect from or about you (including your personal information) from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Please be aware, however, that no method of transmitting information over the Internet or storing information is completely secure.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes to this policy are effective as soon as they are posted on this page. We encourage you to review our Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed about our practices.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hackpost.guide@gmail.com.
