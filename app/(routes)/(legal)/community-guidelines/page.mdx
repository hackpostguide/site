# Hackpost Guide Community Guidelines

Welcome to Hackpost Guide, a community dedicated to creativity, sharing, and collaboration. We're excited to have you here! By participating in this community, you agree to adhere to these Community Guidelines to ensure a positive, inclusive, and safe environment for everyone.

### Our Pledge

In the spirit of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

### Community Conduct

- **Treat everyone with respect.** Embrace the diversity of other users and be considerate of the multitude of views and opinions found in our community.
- **Be safe.** Do not share your personal or contact information, and respect the privacy of others. Discourage the sharing of sensitive details such as real names, phone numbers, locations, email addresses, or social media accounts.
- **Be honest.** Authenticity and honesty contribute to the trust and health of this community. Do not impersonate others or spread misinformation.
- **Keep the community friendly.** Ensure that your contributions are appropriate for all ages. If you encounter negative or harmful behavior, report it in our discord server. Our moderators will handle the situation.

### Prohibited Conduct

- **No harassment or bullying.** This includes sexual harassment or unwelcome sexualized comments, threats, or intimidation.
- **Avoid hate speech.** Do not promote violence or hate against individuals or groups based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual preference, disability, or any other characteristic.
- **No trolling or disruptive comments.** Keep discussions constructive.
- **Respect digital privacy.** Do not publish private information of others without consent.
- **No plagiarism or misappropriation of creative works.** Always credit original creators and respect intellectual property rights.

### Enforcement

Violations of these guidelines may result in removal of content, a temporary ban, or permanent ban from the community, depending on the severity of the breach. To report a violation, please submit a report in the *report* channel in our Discord server.

### Reporting Guidelines

To ensure issues are dealt with efficiently, please provide:
- A clear description of the issue or behavior.
- Any relevant screenshots or links.
- The username(s) or content involved.

### Help Us Keep Hackpost Guide Welcoming

We're committed to upholding these standards and encourage you to contact us with feedback or concerns about community behavior. 
Thank you for being part of Hackpost Guide!
